## 备餐建议（适合一周只买一次菜）

### 1）Staple grain
- Your week's brown rice is already cooked, total cooked weight **3269 g**
- Portion it out by day — easiest approach
- Recommended portions:
  - **You-shrimp × 4 containers: 352 g each**
  - **You-beef × 3 containers: 308 g each**
  - **Wife-shrimp × 4 containers: 145 g each**
  - **Wife-beef × 3 containers: 119 g each**
- Each day, split that container into lunch and dinner portions

### 2）Shrimp
- Bag into daily portions after buying
- Store in the freezer
- Move the next day's portion to the fridge the night before to thaw

### 3）Beef leg
- Bag into daily portions for beef days
- Slice against the grain before portioning
- Can marinate in advance with a little black pepper and a small amount of soy sauce

### 4）Vegetables
- Don't wash all the leafy greens at once
- Process in two batches: first half and second half of the week
- Romaine lettuce, napa cabbage, and bok choy are best eaten earlier in the week
- Tomatoes, green peppers, and shiitake mushrooms are better bought in larger quantities

## 每道菜超简做法

### Day 1｜Tomato Shrimp + Bok Choy
1. Thaw shrimp and drain well.
2. Cut tomatoes into chunks; wash bok choy.
3. Heat pan, add measured oil, stir-fry tomatoes until juicy.
4. Add shrimp and stir-fry until just pink.
5. Stir-fry bok choy separately or toss in at the end.
6. Divide into two portions.

### Day 2｜Green Pepper Beef + Romaine Lettuce
1. Slice beef leg thinly against the grain.
2. Mix with a little soy sauce and black pepper; rest for 10 minutes.
3. Wash and cut romaine lettuce into sections.
4. Heat pan with oil; stir-fry beef quickly until browned, then set aside.
5. Stir-fry green pepper and romaine lettuce quickly.
6. Return beef to pan, toss together, divide into two portions.

### Day 3｜Garlic Shrimp with Bell Pepper + Romaine Lettuce
1. Thaw and drain shrimp; slice bell pepper into strips.
2. Heat pan with oil; sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
3. Add shrimp and stir-fry; then add bell pepper.
4. Stir-fry romaine lettuce separately or toss in quickly.
5. Divide into two portions.

### Day 4｜Shiitake Beef + Napa Cabbage
1. Slice beef thinly; slice shiitake mushrooms; wash napa cabbage.
2. Heat pan with oil; stir-fry beef first.
3. Add shiitake mushrooms and stir-fry until fragrant.
4. Stir-fry napa cabbage separately or add in at the end.
5. Divide into two portions.

### Day 5｜Black Pepper Shrimp + Napa Cabbage
1. Toss shrimp lightly with black pepper.
2. Wash napa cabbage.
3. Heat pan with oil; stir-fry shrimp first.
4. Add napa cabbage and toss; season with a little salt.
5. Divide into lunch and dinner portions.

### Day 6｜Green Pepper Wood-Ear Beef + Romaine Lettuce
1. Slice beef; soak dried wood ear in advance; slice green pepper; wash romaine lettuce.
2. Heat pan with oil; stir-fry beef first.
3. Add wood ear and green pepper; stir-fry quickly.
4. Add romaine lettuce last and toss quickly.
5. Season with a little soy sauce and black pepper; divide into two portions.

### Day 7｜Tomato Shrimp + Baby Cabbage
1. Cut tomatoes into chunks; cut baby cabbage into sections; thaw shrimp.
2. Heat pan with oil; stir-fry tomatoes first.
3. Add shrimp and stir-fry until just cooked.
4. Stir-fry baby cabbage quickly or cover and cook for 1–2 minutes.
5. Divide into two portions.
